MBA

Master’s in Business Administration (MBA) is a postgraduate degree focusing on business administration and investment management. The core courses in an MBA program cover various areas of business administration such as applied statistics, human resources, finance, managerial economics, marketing, supply-chain management, and operations management in a manner most relevant to management analysis and strategy.

Program highlights

1. Practice-based course
Strong Emphasis on practical applications in real life

2. General applications and specialisation
Geared towards a broad range of skills and capacity building in the first two years

3. India-specific case studies
Exposure to diverse global worldviews and narratives, and contrasting them with Indian thinking

4. Interdisciplinary learning
Non-business subjects to gain a broader worldview

5. Experiential learning
Students are encouraged to engage in community activities and develop professional behaviour

6. Working collaboratively
Encouraging groups to do peer-to-peer learning

7. Formative assessments
Evaluation via learning exercises followed by real-time feedback mechanism
